About Us

MATSIPANE PROJECTS(PTY)Ltd is a professional Geosciences and Environmental Consulting firm providing specialist services across South Africa.

Our work supports responsible development within the mining, construction, and agricultural sectors. We combine scientific expertise, field investigations, and advanced modelling techniques to deliver reliable, data-driven solutions.

We are committed to:

  • Regulatory compliance
  • Scientific accuracy
  • Professional integrity
  • Timely project delivery

Our approach integrates technical knowledge with practical implementation, ensuring that each project meets environmental legislation requirements and industry best practices.
