Our Services

At Matsipane Consulting (Pty) Ltd, we provide specialised hydrological and geohydrological consulting services to support sustainable water resource management, regulatory compliance, and environmental protection.

Hydrological Services

Flood Hydrology & Floodline Assessments

We conduct detailed flood hydrology studies and floodline determinations to identify flood-prone areas and support safe infrastructure planning and regulatory compliance.

Salt and Water Balance Assessments

Assessment of water inflows, outflows, and salt balance to support environmental management and operational efficiency in mining and industrial projects.

Water Conservation & Demand Management Plans

Development of strategic water management plans aimed at reducing water consumption and improving resource efficiency.

Hydropedology & Soil Pollution Assessments

Investigation of soil-water interactions and assessment of soil contamination risks to support environmental impact studies and land-use planning.

Stormwater Management Plans

Design and evaluation of stormwater management systems to minimise environmental impacts and ensure sustainable drainage solutions.

Water Quality Assessment & Monitoring

Comprehensive water quality testing, sampling, and reporting to monitor compliance with environmental standards and legislation.

Geohydrological Services

Groundwater Prospecting Surveys

Identification and evaluation of groundwater resources for borehole development and sustainable water supply.

Leachate Plume Assessments & Monitoring

Assessment and monitoring of contaminant plumes to evaluate environmental risks and protect groundwater resources.

Borehole Yield Tests

Scientific testing and analysis to determine sustainable borehole yield and groundwater availability.

Groundwater Modelling

Advanced numerical groundwater modelling to simulate water flow and contaminant transport for risk assessment and project planning.

Water Quality Assessments & Monitoring

Surface and groundwater quality analysis to support environmental compliance and reporting.

Geochemical Modelling for Waste Classes

Geochemical analysis and modelling to classify waste materials and assess environmental risks.

Water Use Licence Applications

Professional support in preparing and submitting Water Use Licence Applications (WULA) in compliance with South African water regulations.
